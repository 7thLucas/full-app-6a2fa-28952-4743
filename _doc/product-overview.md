# Product Overview

## What It Is
A unified wellness platform that enables practitioners to deliver counseling sessions, yoga classes, and webinars — all from one place. Clients access every service through a single, cohesive portal.

## Core Services
- **Counseling Sessions** — 1:1 and group therapy or coaching appointments, bookable and deliverable in-app
- **Yoga Classes** — live or on-demand physical wellness sessions with scheduling and attendance tracking
- **Webinars** — live knowledge-sharing broadcasts for groups and communities

## Users
- **Practitioners / Providers**: Therapists, yoga instructors, coaches, and wellness educators who run multiple service types and need unified booking and delivery tools
- **Clients / Attendees**: Individuals seeking mental wellness (counseling), physical wellness (yoga), and personal growth (webinars) without juggling separate platforms

## Positioning
One platform, three service types. The app eliminates the tool sprawl practitioners face when running counseling, yoga, and webinar businesses separately — replacing 3–5 disconnected tools with a single, cohesive hub.

- Deliver a frictionless single-portal experience for clients across all three service categories
